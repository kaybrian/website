SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

Education

To promote education in Teso sub region through sponsorship of children living in or originating from Teso sub region by promoting free, equal access to quality education for all children

Vocational skills

To build vocational skills training institution for lifelong learning and any other educational or related institutions in Teso sub region.

Donate Now

Scholastic

To provide children with scholastic materials, such as books, and other basic social needs that they need so as to continue with their Education

Donate Now

Transformation

To promote tree and fruit planting and growing bird and biodiversity conservation to eradicate poverty and transform people’s livelihoods of the people that work and live in Teso sub region.

Biodiversity

To promote environmental and biodiversity conservation in Teso sub region by advocating for suitable utilization of natural and artificial forest resources.
