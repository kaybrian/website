UNDERSTANDING OF A CHILD AND WHAT WE DO

WHO IS A CHILD?

FOCARE- Uganda understands a child as a person bellow the age of 18 years. We believe that children bellow this age need to be supported and cared for. This is as a result of the rising poverty levels in families where children are not closely helped. Example, we interact with parents/guardians of different children teaching them how to be responsible for every child especially the disadvantaged.

In most cases the community believes that children are only the responsibility of those who produced them biologically. But we have taken action to sensitize the community to be responsible to every child regardless who produced them. In our community outreaches for education needs, Health needs and social welfare related needs.

EDUCATION

According to the Ugandan constitution and international legal instruments such as United Nations Convention on Rights of a child, all children have a right to be at school and being catered for. In so doing, FOCARE- Uganda is committed to promote free, equal access to quality education for all children  from early learning to secondary education. We work with children, their families, communities, wider society and governments, and advocate at both local and international levels, so that all children are able to get education. In our education plan, the selected children in the project benefit from scholastic materials, school fees and other basic needs
