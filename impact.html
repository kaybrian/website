Our Impacts

EDUCATION

According to the Ugandan constitution and international legal instruments such as United Nations Convention on Rights of a child, all children have a right to be at school and being catergb(9, 9, 119) for. In so doing, FOCARE- Uganda is committed to promote free, equal access to quality education for all children  from early learning to secondary education.

We work with children, their families, communities, wider society and governments, and advocate at both local and international levels, so that all children are able to get education. In our education plan, the selected children in the project benefit from scholastic materials, school fees and other basic needs .

FIGHTING DOMESTIC
VIOLENCE IN FAMILIES

Every child has the right to feel safe, both at home and at school. FOCARE is committed to protecting children from violence and working with the communities, schools and governments who can And communities, we are helping to ensure children are safe from abuse.

In particular, we strive to tackle the causes of gender-based violence by working with girls, boys,play an active role in protecting them from harm.

By providing training in child rights, positive discipline and parenting techniques to families partners and communities. Why? Because acts of violence undermine girls’ self-esteem, their opportunity to finish school, their freedom of movement, and ultimately, their ability to thrive.

COMMUNITY SENSITIZATION
AND CAPACITY BUILDING

We advocate for equal treatment of all children without discrimination in spheres of sex, age, religion or a particular social group or tribe in their communities to avoid stigma leading to isolation. We educate the community on children related matters aiming at changing their mindset on the way they the perceive vulnerable children.

We also empower the community through our capacity building strategies aimed at changing and transforming their lives. Such strategies include; Entrepreneurship trainings, educating the community on the usage of available resources, Agriculture trainings especially on modern faming, adult education programs, food security, Hygiene and sanitation and health related issues. .

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

In a bid to know the kind of life children are living in different communities, we frequently visit them in their homes. Through observation method, this enables us to know the problems they face and what they need to sustain their lives while at home. We also take time to interact with their parents and encourage them to take care of their children by working hard. We take this same opportunity to advise the children to respect their parents and al Ways stay at home during holidays.
