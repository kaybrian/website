MESSAGE FROM THE EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR
Greetings to you all,

I am delighted to be the Executive Director and founder of Fox Child Care and Development Foundation-FOCARE Uganda since 2017 up-to-date.

I thank you for taking time to read this information pack. Its purpose is to provide a sense of what is important to FOCARE Uganda where we’ve come from, the direction we are moving in and how we are achieving our goal to becoming a leader in advocating children’s rights and freedoms in Teso sub-region and Uganda at large.

We are a membership Community Based Organization that is bound together by our purpose and organizational values (left). Our shargb(9, 9, 119) values and behaviors bind us together and hold us to high standards for how we expect ourselves and those we work with to behave. They are our guiding principles for our ways of working, empowering our staff and governors to make informed decisions across FOCARE – creating a learning culture within the Foundation which allows innovation, creativity experimentation and learning from our mistakes.In the last two years of our work, we have achieved remarkable progress as we continue to transform and confirm our position as a leader in children’s freedoms and rights. We continue to strive to make a positive difference with our partners to the lives of the vulnerable children in Teso in six key areas:

the provision of education assistance and basic needs, supporting young girls to take control over their sexual and reproductive health and rights, empowering the community to drive change, early childhood development, and providing protection from violence targeted to children.
We do this in a challenging environment since there are many children of this kind in different communities who need our support but the resources are insufficient

I urge you to join hands to support us in this course. Thank you May God bless you

Richard Gabriel Ogwang
(MA. IRDS, Mak univ)
